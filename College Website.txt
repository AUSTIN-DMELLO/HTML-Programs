<caption align="center"><font color="blue"><h1>PACE Junior Science College</h2></font></caption>
<marque direction="right">
<p>Seven years ago, IIT-ian's Pace introduced the unique concept of integrated learning for Competitive
Exams, Board Exams through partnerships with the most reputed schools and colleges across Mumbai.</p>
<p>In 2009, the Government of Maharashtra entrusted IIT-ians's Pace with the responsibility to manage two
junior colleges in collaboration with the MCGM. these colleges offer integrated Competitive Exam
preparation and Board preparation umder one roof.</p>
<p>Today, the success of this concept has led to a revolution for competitive exams and boards. In a
landmark ruling, <u>the Government of Maharashtra awarded IIT-ian's Pace</u> special permission to run 6
Pace Junior Science colleges across Mumbai</p>
